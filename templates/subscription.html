Access & Pricing

Healthcare should be accessible — not locked behind a paywall.

100% Free Access

This platform does not use a subscription model. All essential health services are available without recurring payments.

  • ✔ Mental Health & Reproductive Health support
  • ✔ Hospital & Government-integrated services
  • ✔ Appointment demos & doctor connect
  • ✔ Multilingual, nationwide accessibility
  • ✔ Inclusive, gender-neutral design

Optional paid products may be available through the Health Shopping Library (such as wellness items or future wearable devices), but core healthcare services will always remain free.
